Salesai

Intelligent Sales Assistant Platform

A complete AI-powered sales platform for automated customer service via Telegram

📋 Product Overview

Salesai is a modern, cloud-native sales automation platform that enables businesses to:
  • ✅ Automate Customer Interactions - AI agent handles customer conversations 24/7
  • ✅ Manage Products & Inventory - Intuitive dashboard for sellers
  • ✅ Process Orders Intelligently - Automatic discount calculation and order processing
  • ✅ Integrate with Telegram - Reach customers where they are
  • ✅ Scale Effortlessly - Serverless AWS infrastructure

🎯 Problem Statement

Traditional e-commerce challenges: 1. ❌ Sellers can't respond to customers 24/7 2. ❌ Manual discount calculation is error-prone 3. ❌ Multiple platforms are difficult to manage 4. ❌ High operational costs for customer service 5. ❌ Limited automation capabilities Our Solution: ✅ AI-powered agent handles customer inquiries automatically ✅ Smart discount engine calculates best prices ✅ Single platform for product and order management ✅ Serverless architecture reduces costs by 70% ✅ Complete automation with human oversight
